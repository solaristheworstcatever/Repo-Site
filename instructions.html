Compilations

1: Extract the downloaded zip

Select the level editor you're using:

Community / Official
Henry's
Rained
RWE+ / Ogscule

2: Open your leditor's initial folder

2: From the initial folder of your leditor go through folders /assets/renderer/

2: If you've installed Rained with previously existing leditor folder, go to its destination
Otherwise:
From the initial folder of your leditor go to the /Data/ folder

2: From the initial folder of your leditor go through folders /drizzle/Data/

3: Insert the contents of the zip and hit "replace"
